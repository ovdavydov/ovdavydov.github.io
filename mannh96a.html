O.Davydov, M.Sommer and H.Strauss, On almost interpolation by multivariate splines, in "Multivariate Approximation and Splines" (G.Nürnberger, J.W.Schmidt and G.Walz, Eds.), pp. 45-58, ISNM, Birkhäuser, 1997.

Abstract: A survey on some recent developments in multivariate interpolation, including characterizations of almost interpolation sets with respect to finite-dimensional spaces by conditions of Schoenberg-Whitney type, is given.
